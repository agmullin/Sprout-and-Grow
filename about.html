About Sprout & Grow

Sprout & Grow is Raleigh’s premier destination for organic gardening. Founded on the principles of sustainability, we aim to educate and empower our community with expert advice and resources.

From home gardeners to urban green enthusiasts, we’re committed to fostering a love for sustainable gardening and preserving the environment.
